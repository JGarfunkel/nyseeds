Chapter 63 Freshwater Wetlands

[HISTORY: Adopted by the Town Board of the Town of Pound Ridge 8-14-1986 as L.L. No. 1-1986.[1] Amendments noted where applicable.)
GENERAL REFERENCES
Conservation Board — See Ch. 4.
Flood damage prevention — See Ch. 60.
Wells — See Ch. 108.
Zoning — See Ch. 113.
Land development — See Ch. A117.
[1]
Editor's Note: The preface of this local law reads as follows:
Pursuant to Section 24-0501 of the New York State Freshwater Wetlands Act (Article 24 of the New York Environmental Conservation Law) and in accordance with 6 NYCRR 665, the Town of Pound Ridge has developed this chapter regulating the dredging, filling, deposition or removal of materials, the diversion or obstruction of water flow, the placement of structures, and other uses in the ponds. lakes. reservoirs, natural drainage systems and wetlands and the requirement of permits therefor; and establishing a Water Control Commission in the Town of Pound Ridge."
