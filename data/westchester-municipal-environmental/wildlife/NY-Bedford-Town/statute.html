  1. Conservation Board

    Not a permitting board, but a body that often weighs in on conservation aspects of land use applications, the Bedford Conservation Board is committed to working locally to improve the environmental health of all of our community and to foster a resilient future for our town and the world.

    Subdivisions, site plans, steep slopes permits, and special use permits may be approved by this body made up of local residents.
