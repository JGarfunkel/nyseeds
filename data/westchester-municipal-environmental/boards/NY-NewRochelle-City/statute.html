Article XXVII Ecology and Natural Resources Advisory Committee
[Adopted 7-9-2019 by Res. No. 2019-131; amended in its entirety 10-15-2019 by Res. No. 2019-212]
§ 9-99 Committee established.
chevron_right
§ 9-99.1 Auxiliary youth members.
chevron_right
[Amended 5-17-2022 by Ord. No. 2022-60]
This City Council hereby establishes an Advisory Committee on Ecology and Natural Resources consisting of up to 18 members appointed by and serving at the pleasure of the City Manager, including a Chair appointed by and serving at the pleasure of the City Manager.[1]
[1]
Editor's Note: Resolution No. 42-2022, adopted 4-19-2022, designated the Ecology and Natural Resources Advisory Committee as the City's official Climate Smart Community Task Force and designated the Sustainability Coordinator as the Climate Smart Community Coordinator.
[Added 1-21-2025 by Res. No. 2025-14]
The Council of the City of New Rochelle hereby authorizes the City Manager to appoint three Auxiliary Youth Members to ENRAC, subject to the following criteria:
A. 
Candidates must be residents of New Rochelle under the age 20.
B. 
Candidates must receive endorsements from the New Rochelle City School District Green Schools Committee and ENRAC.
C. 
Auxiliary Youth Board Members will serve one-year terms and be selected through a competitive application process.
