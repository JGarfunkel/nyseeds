§ 10-7

Creation; powers and duties; membership. 

The Yorktown Conservation Board, created pursuant
to § 239-x of the General Municipal Law, shall have all
the powers as set forth therein and shall consist of 10 members who
shall be appointed by the Town Board of the Town of Yorktown. Said
members of the Conservation Board shall serve at the pleasure of the
Town Board for a term not exceeding three years.
