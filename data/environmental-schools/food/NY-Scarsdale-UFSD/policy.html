Mac OS User Tabbing

For Mac OS users, there is a system setting that may not allow you to tab onto several types of elements in a web page. To change this setting:

Open System Preferences and go to Keyboard:
Select the Shortcuts tab. Make sure the box for "Use keyboard navigation to move focus between controls" is checked.
Additional settings for Safari Browser.
Open Safari Preferences:
Select the Advanced tab. Make sure the box for "Press Tab to highlight each item on a webpage" is checked.

Sustainability Initiatives

Sustainability Initiatives

In conjunction with the district’s Sustainability Committee, the Food Service Department is dedicated to providing meals that not only keep our students happy and healthy, but also keep the wellbeing of our environment at the forefront. Sustainability measures taken include:

  • Compostable trays, utensils, milk straws, soup and coffee cups, bowls, boxes, and napkins at all schools
  • Recycling of appropriate products
  • Reusable dinnerware in the Learning Commons Café at the High School
  • Utilization of reusable trays, pans, and service flatware for our satellite Elementary School program
  • Purchasing of local products, when available
