NEWS! Sept. 2024 Sustainability Update

SustainabilityOur goal is to educate for a sustainable future.

Board of Education Subcommittee

The Sustainability Committee is a sub-committee of the Board of Education with the mission of educating for a sustainable future by focusing on district campuses, community and curriculum. The committee's goals include: 
reducing the district's environmental impacts
promoting awareness of environmental and sustainability issues among our community
encouraging consideration of sustainability in individual and collective decision making.

Time outdoors, sustainability clubs, environmental science classes and the District's commitment to reducing emissions equip learners with the knowledge, skills and motivation to address complex environmental challenges.  








2007-2008: KLSD Board of Education appoints a Sustainability Committee

2008 - 2009:  Sustainability Committee conducts greenhouse gas inventory

2011 - 2012: The District commits to 7 million dollar, district-wide energy performance contract 

2012 - 2013:  All KLSD elementary schools take food scraps out of waste stream

2013 - 2016: John Jay Middle School implements food scrap composting program.

2016 - 2017: KLSD reduces greenhouse gas by 24% and paper use by about 40%. 

2018-2019: KLSD makes significant gains on composting and recycling.
